Ratatoskr plays your Audiobookshelf audiobooks on Sonos and IKEA SYMFONISK speakers,
and keeps your listening progress in sync.

This app is a remote for a Ratatoskr server running on your own network. Sign in with your
Audiobookshelf account, browse your library, pick a speaker, and control playback. The
audio streams directly from your Audiobookshelf server to the speaker, so your phone does
not have to be the source: no drained battery, no interruption when a call comes in, and
you can leave the room.

On Android there is no native AirPlay and Sonos does not support Google Cast, so this is
the missing link between Audiobookshelf and your speakers.

Requires a running Ratatoskr server and an Audiobookshelf account.

project.
