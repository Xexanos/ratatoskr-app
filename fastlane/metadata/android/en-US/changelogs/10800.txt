The library now shows a docked mini player whenever a book is playing: cover, title, speaker, and a position that ticks forward smoothly instead of jumping every few seconds, with a one-tap pause/resume and a tap to open the full player. Session state is now tracked in one place across the whole app, so the "Continue listening" shelf also quietly refreshes the moment a book stops playing while you're on the library. The Speakers screen always shows the freshest speaker names now too.
